About Us

Our mission is to make sure that no good food goes to waste while millions go hungry. By using AI and smart technology, we connect food donors—like restaurants, supermarkets, and caterers—with NGOs and shelters that need it the most. We aim to build a seamless, transparent, and scalable system that makes food redistribution easy, efficient, and impactful. Together, we can turn surplus food into meals for those in need and take a step closer to a hunger-free India.
